# 2. BUSINESS STRATEGY & MODEL

### What the site IS
A **showcase + conversion funnel.** It persuades, sorts visitors by level, and sends them to checkout.

### What the site is NOT
A learning platform. No student dashboard, no video hosting, no LMS. All learning happens via WhatsApp + Notion. (This keeps launch fast and cheap.)

### The three jobs at launch
1. **Sell courses** — via Salla checkout
2. **Collect leads** — Free Test, Speaking Test, WhatsApp, "Notify me"
3. **Look credible** — 5,000+ beneficiaries, testimonials, professional brand

### The funnel engine
**The Free Level Test.** It gives value, captures the lead, sorts every visitor by CEFR level, and funnels them to the exact course to buy. Everything else supports this.

### Revenue model
- **Per-course ticket sales** through Salla (not subscription at launch)
- Natural upsell ladder: A0 → A1 → A2 → B1 → B2
- Potential future: Speaking Test as paid product, bundles

### 🗣️ Conversational Stream — 5 Stages
1. **Frame** — teacher states steps, sets up scenario (EN + AR)
2. **Vocabulary** — 🔵 BASIC + 🔴 ADVANCED words, each with example sentence, translated
3. **Dialogue** — two-person conversation; teacher reads one part, viewer reads other aloud; both translated
4. **Unscripted drill** — question + sentence-starters; Arabic suggested answer → render in English → English revealed
5. **AI practice** — student pastes ready-made prompt into AI tool to rehearse live (AI explains in Arabic, asks in English, corrects each answer, feedback in Arabic)

Example scenarios: asking directions, visiting a friend, café, airport, etc.
